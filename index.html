Nail It The perfect app to make your nails look stunning! If you ever thought that having plain red nails just looks extremely boring, then this app is exactly for you! Nail It is the only app of its kind! The designs are specifically chosen so that they can be done by yourself at home in 10 mins. The designs are divided into packs and each has its own theme, so there is something to be found for everyone; from casual to crazy party designs and from black elegant styles to cute and adorable ones. Each pack can be purchased inside the app.
